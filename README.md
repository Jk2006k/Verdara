# Verdara

Verdara is an interactive Android live wallpaper that brings a living tree to your home screen. The tree grows as your phone charges and adapts to real-world conditions by changing its environment based on the weather, time of day, and device state. Verdara transforms your wallpaper into a small, living ecosystem that evolves with you and your surroundings.

## Tech Stack

* **Frontend & Mobile Development:** Flutter
* **Native Android Integration:** Kotlin, Android SDK
* **Animations & Graphics:** Flame, Rive
* **Backend (Yet to be decided):** Django, Django REST Framework
* **Database (Yet to be decided):** PostgreSQL
* **APIs:** OpenWeather API, WeatherAPI
